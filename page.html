About Sarah

Expert reformer with a heart for rescue dogs

Certified & Compassionate

With over 12 years of hands-on experience in dog rehabilitation, Sarah specializes in transforming the lives of rescue dogs. Her comprehensive approach combines behavioral training, trauma therapy, and medical care to help dogs overcome past traumas.

Sarah's mission is simple: every dog deserves a second chance. She works closely with Go Puppy to identify dogs that need rehabilitation and matches them with families who have the patience and love to support their recovery journey.
